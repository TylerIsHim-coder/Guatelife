Our mission

Talent is everywhere.
Opportunity is not.

Across Guatemala, brilliant students are held back not by ability, but by access. GuateLife closes that gap — connecting them with mentors, knowledge, and a global community that believes in their potential.

How it works

Three steps to a changed life.

Step 01

Match

We pair each student with a mentor aligned to their goals, language, and ambitions.

Step 02

Mentor

Weekly sessions, curated Khan Academy paths, and real human guidance build momentum.

Step 03

Mobilize

Students unlock scholarships, universities, and careers — then return to lift the next.
